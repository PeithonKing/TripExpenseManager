Trip Expense Manager

How to Use

Step 1: Add People Involved

Click on the "Add Person" button to add all the people involved in this project. You can add more people later too.

Step 2: Add Expense Rows

Click on the "Add Row" button to add rows for each expense.

Filling the Rows:
  • The first three columns are self-explanatory:
    • Expense Name: Enter the name of the expense
    • Amount Spent: Enter the total amount spent on this expense
    • Spent By: Choose one person from the list of people you added earlier to mark who spent this amount
  • The rest of the columns are people-specific columns, representing each person's share of the expense.

You can fill the columns in two ways:

  1. Ratio Method: Enter a ratio value (e.g., 1, 2, 3) for each person. The system will automatically calculate the share based on this ratio. (if you want equal division, put 1 against everyone's names, if some person shouldn't share some expense, you can put 0 against their names)
  2. Exact Amount Method: Enter the exact amount to be paid by each person. Make sure these amounts add up to the total "Amount Spent" value.

Note: Behind the scenes, the system multiplies all share values by a factor that ensures they add up to the total "Amount Spent" value.
